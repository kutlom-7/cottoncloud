Vanilla French Toast

Recipe:
Ingredients:

  • 4 slices brioche
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
Method:
  1. Whisk eggs, milk, and vanilla together.
  2. Dip bread slices and fry until golden brown.
  3. Serve with syrup or fresh fruits.

Strawberry Daiquiri

Recipe:
Ingredients:

  • 100g strawberries
  • 50ml white rum
  • 25ml lime juice
  • 15ml simple syrup
Method:
  1. Blend all ingredients with ice.
  2. Serve chilled in a glass with strawberry garnish.

Curtain Care

Care Guide:
- Vacuum curtains weekly.
- Wash or dry clean depending on fabric.
- Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading.
- Iron on low heat if needed.
